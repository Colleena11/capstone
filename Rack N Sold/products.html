How to Upload Your Artwork

×

1. Select Your Image

Click the upload area or drag and drop your artwork image. Make sure it's clear and high-quality.

2. Add Title and Details

Give your artwork a title and add your artist name. These help buyers find your work.

3. Set Your Price

Set a fair price for your artwork (minimum ₱100). Consider your time, materials, and market value.

4. Write Description

Describe your artwork, including size, materials, and any special features that make it unique.
